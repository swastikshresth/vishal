Our Community

The Tech community, Alpha Sanctuary has been made, keeping in mind, how as engineering students its important for us to have a platform to exchange ideas, meet with new people for building long lasting teams and work on different projects with different teams.

Description

By joining Alpha Sanctuary, you'll be able to find different people from different background and have an opportunity to be part of a strong community that has been built solely for the purpose of exchange of ideas ,improvement of skills and team building.

The purpose simply put is for us to brainstorm together on different projects and ideas and work on them to gain experience and learn. I hope you'll be able to find people to share ideas with or work with on this channel.

AlphaDevs is a group of like-minded people working towards the goal of leveraging technology to make the world around them better.
